# Klerk landing page

A single-page, brand-native pitch built for Ally Wolodarsky and Tom Bitton of Klerk, tuned to the concierge-layer orchestration hypothesis in the Klerk fit analysis and dressed in Klerk's own design language: navy #1f3247 surfaces, the signature magenta gradient on the highlighted headline phrase, pill CTA, Euclid Circular B with Inter as the fallback (already in Klerk's live stylesheet), subtle elevation and a pale #eff7ff section wash. The hero carries the fit-analysis headline verbatim, the 60/30/10 frame is broken out as three cards so the CTO can take it in at a glance, the Feeld case and the ICM paper each get their own surface, and the CTA panel points at Matt Creamer's Calendly. No pricing, no retainer terms, no em dashes, no performed questions. Mobile-first, tested at 375px, and the only Eduba mark appears in the footer.
